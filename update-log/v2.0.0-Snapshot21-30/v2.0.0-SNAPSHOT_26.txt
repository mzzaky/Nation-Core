====================================================
  INVOICE-BASED NATION TAX SYSTEM OVERHAUL
====================================================
Replaced the legacy direct-deduction tax mechanism with a fully invoice-based billing system, split the centralized TaxGUI into four per-nation tax menus (including the brand-new Caliphate "Zakah"), and introduced a permanent national-debt forced-collection mechanic.

[NEW INVOICE TAX MECHANISM]
- TaxInvoice.java (id.nationcore.models) — NEW FILE:
  → Invoice model with lifecycle ACTIVE → OVERDUE → NATIONAL_DEBT → PAID.
  → Each invoice remembers its issuing nation (id + name), base amount ($50), paid amount, penalty flag, and timestamps.
  → Constants: 1-day due period, 3-day national-debt deadline (counted from issue time), 2x penalty multiplier.
- TaxRecord.java (id.nationcore.models) — REWRITTEN:
  → Removed all legacy structures: PlayerTaxData (outstanding debt, missed payments, punishment history, exemptions) and the TaxTransaction global history.
  → New per-player PlayerTaxProfile: invoice list, Auto Pay preference, payment log (keeps last 20), and lifetime statistics (invoices issued / paid on time / paid with penalty / defaulted, totals for tax, penalties, and debt repaid).
  → Added sequential invoice numbering (INV-00001, ...), billing-cycle counter, and global collection totals (tax / penalties / seized debt).
  → ensureInitialized() recreates collections missing when loading data files written by the old system.
- TaxManager.java (id.nationcore.managers) — REWRITTEN:
  → Billing cycle: every 1 real-life day the system generates a $50 tax invoice for every nation member (players offline 3+ days are skipped so bills do not pile up); NPC fake-member collection is preserved each cycle.
  → Legacy collection mechanism removed entirely: no more direct balance deduction at collection time, percentage late-penalty on debt, missed-payment counters, tax-evasion punishments, exemptions, or admin debt forgiveness.
  → Penalty mechanism (new): an invoice unpaid for 1 day after generation becomes OVERDUE — its value doubles to $100 (penalty category). Checked every minute with instant player notification.
  → National debt mechanism (new): an invoice still unpaid 3 days after generation becomes NATIONAL_DEBT — its value is recorded permanently and the player is notified with a title warning.
  → Forced bypass collection: every minute (and on every join) the state seizes whatever Vault balance a debtor holds — partial seizure allowed — until the debt is settled. Works while offline and even after the player leaves the issuing nation, since invoices remember their nation; future earnings keep being seized until the invoice is fully paid.
  → Invoices belonging to dissolved nations are voided automatically (no treasury remains to receive the funds).
  → Auto Pay: per-player toggle; while enabled, each newly issued invoice is paid instantly from the player's Vault balance.
  → payAllInvoices(): settles every affordable outstanding invoice oldest-first; every payment is withdrawn from the player's Vault balance and deposited into the issuing nation's treasury as TAX_INCOME.
  → Player notifications for issued / auto-paid / doubled / defaulted invoices and seizures, plus a server-wide billing summary broadcast each cycle.

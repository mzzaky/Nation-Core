====================================================
  NATION CORE - UPDATE LOG v2.0.0-SNAPSHOT#21
====================================================
Added Minister of Health position to Republic nations, implemented its cabinet decisions, and updated Republic Main Menu.

[REPUBLIC CABINET & MAIN MENU UPDATES]
- RepublicMainMenu.java:
  → Changed buildCabinetCard signature to accept Nation object.
  → Updated Cabinet card lore to display all three positions (Defense, Treasury, Health), current minister names, and any active cabinet decisions/orders with remaining duration.
  → Disabled click actions visual indicator from the Cabinet card lore.
  → Imported CabinetDecision.
  → Changed President Election item material from LIGHT_BLUE_BUNDLE to ENDER_EYE.
- RepublicGUIHandler.java:
  → Added slot check for "RESEARCH" to open the Main Research GUI.
  → Removed click handler for "CABINET" slot to disable cabinet menu open click action.

[MINISTER OF HEALTH & CABINET DECISIONS]
- Government.java:
  → Added HEALTH ("Minister of Health", "&d[MoH]") to CabinetPosition enum.
- CabinetDecision.java:
  → Added HEALTH position to CabinetPosition enum.
  → Added 5 Health-related decisions to DecisionType: QUARANTINE_PROTOCOL, FIELD_MEDICINE, VACCINATION_DRIVE, EMERGENCY_RATIONS, and PLAGUE.
- CabinetManager.java:
  → Added support for mapping the 5 Health decisions to the HEALTH position.
  → Added display names for all new Health decisions.
  → Implemented overloaded isDecisionActive(Nation, DecisionType) to check per-nation decision active state.
  → Implemented action effects for the 5 Health decisions (Regeneration II buff, feeding low hunger players, state broadcasts).
- DamageListener.java:
  → Integrated VACCINATION_DRIVE for Republic, neutralizing poison and wither damage for online nation members when active.
- CapitalListener.java:
  → Integrated QUARANTINE_PROTOCOL for Republic, preventing non-members from entering nation territory when active.
  → Integrated PLAGUE for Republic, applying Weakness II and Hunger to enemy intruders when active.
- CabinetGUI.java:
  → Expanded MINISTER_SLOTS to 3 slots ({ 13, 14, 15 }) to support Defense, Treasury, and Health ministers.
  → Added custom description for the HEALTH minister slot.

[LOCALIZATION & ROUTER]
- MainMenuRouter.java:
  → Translated all Javadoc comments and player-facing string messages from Indonesian to English to ensure codebase localization consistency.
